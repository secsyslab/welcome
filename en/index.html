Network Security Laboratory Home Page

As the development of IoT and AI technologies, information communication systems are more sophisticated. Therefore, the risk of information security is increasing. In the laboratory, we are researching next-generation network systems, authentication protocols, network anomaly detection, and privacy protection technologies as security countermeasure technologies to prevent cyber-attacks. The design of security schemes is a challenge to optimize both security and convenience/efficiency. To solve this problem, we analyze the risk of the target system and design security measures that eliminate the risk from both theoretical and implementation perspectives.
