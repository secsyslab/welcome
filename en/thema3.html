Research area

Privacy and Trust

　

概要

Traffic data over networks may leak personal information, such as location information and products purchased at online stores. Even if the traffic data are encrypted, attackers can obtain personal information from various side channel information. In addition, because the users of the Internet are anonymized, the information sending by anonymized internet users cannot be trusted. Recently, false information, so called fake news, etc. on social networking services and impersonating other people by means of fake video images, known as deepfakes, have also become problems. In big data era, if the data are not trustworthy, the results of that analysis are also not trustworthy. In this way, it is necessary to ensure the reliability of the data flowing through the network.
In this research area, we are studying technologies to prevent privacy leaks from data flowing over the network and to ensure the trustworthy of data themselves.

Study Items

  • Evaluation the trustworthy of data over network
  • Privacy protection to prevent information leakage from encrypted traffic data
  • Privacy policy management for network users
