Research Items

IoT devices such as smart speakers, drones, wearable devices are widely penetrated over mobile network. Furthermore, 6G mobile networks are scheduled to be commercialized by 2030. In addition, AI technology is making remarkable progress, and all kinds of information and communication technologies can be advanced using AI technology. On these networks, a variety of new services are expected to emerge, including automated driving, telemedicine, healthcare, and industrial IoT. On the other hand, security and privacy issues have been concerned in these new networks and services. The research theme of our laboratory is security measures to solve the above issues.
