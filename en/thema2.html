Research area

Attack detection and mesures using AI technology

Summary

With the recent advances in AI technology, for example, deep learning, it can also be applied to measures for cyber-attack. On the other hand, AI technology has become a powerful tool for attackers as well as a tool for security measures. They use AI technology to find vulnerabilities and automatically launch cyber-attacks. Thus, cyberspace is expected to become a confrontation between attacker AI and defender AI soon.
In this theme, we will study the methods for early detection of cyber attacks by analyzing flow data over networks or open data on the Internet.

Study Items

  • Analysis of cyber attacks using network traffic data
  • Analysis of cyber threat information using social media and other public information on the Internet
  • Automatic cyber attack protection
